Surprisingly, Lopunny is the first to succumb to the harsh environment.

The salt has driven moisture out of the soles on her feet. The once soft paw pads have become cracked, hard, calloused and bloody from the day's march.

Swole treats them with potion, a moisturizing salve that Dawn insisted he would need, binds them up in bandages, and finally crafts a pair of shoes to the best of his abilities.

"Good grief. I wish Akari was here. She could have made proper boots for you." He also really misses her. But the utilitarian reason is the only one he is willing to say out loud.

"At least, Salandit was able to find her crafting supplies in my backpack. I had no idea she kept some of it there."

Akari stores crafting supplies everywhere, for crafting related emergencies.

After solving their current emergency, they get up on their feet once more and restart their journey.

Walk, eat, drink, rest, kiss, seethe, sleep, repeat.

For three days, nothing changes.

... Until, they find a little island in the middle of it all.

A tiny thing, but oh-so obvious within their monotonous world.

The siren's island beckons our group closer.

"Let's check it out."
